The Story

Paisley Lifestyle is inspired by the paisley motif — a symbol that has traveled across centuries, cultures, and forms of expression.

Its origins trace back over 2,000 years to ancient Persia, where a teardrop-shaped symbol — known as boteh — represented life, eternity, and renewal. The form echoed nature: a young cypress tree bent by the wind, a flame, or a seed in motion. It was not decorative excess — it was symbolic elegance.

It later evolved in Kashmir through exquisite handwoven textiles, and eventually reached Europe, where it became known as paisley — a mark of refinement and cultivated taste.

At Paisley Lifestyle, we draw from this philosophy.

We believe refinement should feel natural, not rigid. And every detail should contribute to a deeper experience.
